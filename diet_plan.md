# Six-Pack Abs Diet Plan

## User Profile

- **Current Weight:** 100 kg
- **Height:** 6'2"
- **Body Fat:** > 30%
- **Goal:** Six-pack abs (Requires reducing body fat to ~10-12%)

## Strategy Overview

To achieve visible abs, you need to shed significant body fat while maintaining muscle mass. This requires a caloric deficit, high protein intake, and consistent training.

### Nutritional Guidelines

1.  **Caloric Deficit:** Your estimated maintenance calories are around 2,800 - 3,000 kcal. Aim for a target of **2,200 - 2,400 kcal** per day to lose weight steadily (0.5 - 1 kg per week).
2.  **High Protein:** Crucial for muscle retention and satiety. Aim for **2.0 - 2.2g of protein per kg of body weight** (approx. 200g - 220g daily).
3.  **Moderate Carbs:** Focus on complex carbs around workouts.
4.  **Healthy Fats:** Essential for hormone regulation.
5.  **Hydration:** Drink 4-5 liters of water daily.

## Meal Plan Menu (Mix & Match)

_Note: **You can mix freely!** You are NOT tied to the same letter for every meal. For example, you can have Breakfast Option A, Lunch Option C, and Dinner Option B. Just pick one option per meal slot._

### Pre-Workout (5:45 AM)

- **Fuel:** Black Coffee (Caffeine for metabolism) + 1/2 Banana (optional, if needed).
- **Supplements:** **Arginine** (Take 30 mins before gym for better blood flow/pump).
- **Action:** **GO TO GYM @ 6:00 AM**

### Post-Workout Breakfast (8:00 AM) - Options

_Takje **MuscleBlaze MB-Vite** + **Fish Gold** with this meal._

- **Option A (Eggs):** 3 whole eggs + 3 egg whites Scramble/Omelet with spinach/peppers + 1 slice whole-wheat toast.
- **Option B (Oats):** "Protein Oatmeal" - 1/2 cup oats cooked in water, let cool slightly, mix in 1 scoop Whey Protein + berries.
- **Option C (Veg):** 150g Low-fat Paneer Bhurji (lots of veggies) + 1 small Chapati (no oil/ghee).

### Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)

- **Option 1:** 1 scoop of Whey Protein in water.
- **Option 2:** 1 fruit (Apple/Pear/Orange) + 10 Almonds.
- **Option 3:** 3 Boiled Egg Whites.

### Lunch (1:30 PM) - Options

- **Option A (Classic):** 150g-200g Grilled Chicken Breast or Fish + 1 cup Brown Rice + Big Salad.
- **Option B (Bowl):** 1 cup Quinoa + 150g Minced Chicken/Turkey (cooked with low oil/spices) + Steamed Broccoli.
- **Option C (Desi):** 1 Bowl Yellow Dal (Tadka with 1 tsp ghee/oil max) + 100g Grilled Tofu/Paneer + Cucumber Salad (Limit rice to 1/2 cup).

### Afternoon Snack (4:30 PM)

- **Option 1:** Greek Yogurt (unflavored) with a dash of cinnamon/berries.
- **Option 2:** 1 scoop Whey Protein (Chocolate) in water.
- **Option 3:** Green Tea + small bowl of Roasted Chana (chickpeas).

### Dinner (7:30 PM) - Low Carb Focus

_Avoid heavy carbs like rice/roti here to maximize fat burning overnight._

- **Option A:** 150g Lean Meat (Chicken Leg/Beef/Salmon) + Asparagus/Green Beans.
- **Option B:** Large Salad Bowl with 3 Hard Boiled Eggs (or Tuna) + Olive Oil & Vinegar dressing.
- **Option C:** Stir-Fry: Tofu or Chicken chunks tossed with bell peppers, mushrooms, broccoli in soy/garlic sauce.

### Before Bed (Optional - if hungry)

- **Supplement:** **Glutamine** (5g) - Great for recovery and immunity.
- **Snack:** Casein protein shake or 100g Cottage Cheese (slow-digesting protein).

## Important Rules

1.  **No Sugary Drinks:** Eliminate soda, fruit juices, and alcohol.
2.  **Cheat Meal:** One controlled cheat meal per week (not a cheat day) after the first 2 weeks.
3.  ** Consistency:** Meal prep on Sundays to avoid bad choices during the week.
4.  **Sleep:** Aim for 7-8 hours. Lack of sleep increases cortisol, which holds onto belly fat.
