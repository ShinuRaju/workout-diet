# Workout Routine (6:00 AM Start)

**Focus:** Fat Loss + Muscle Retention. Keep rest periods short (45-60 sec) to keep heart rate up.

## Schedule

- **Mon:** Chest + Triceps + Abs
- **Tue:** Back + Biceps + 20m Cardio
- **Wed:** Legs + Shoulders
- **Thu:** Active Recovery / LISS Cardio (45 mins walk/jog) + Abs
- **Fri:** Full Body Compound
- **Sat:** HIIT Cardio or Sports (Swimming/Cycling)
- **Sun:** Rest

## Measurements

- **Warm-up:** 5 mins brisk walk/light jog + Dynamic stretching.
- **Cardio:** Do 15-20 mins _after_ weights, not before (save energy for lifting).

## Detailed Exercises

### Monday: Chest, Triceps, Abs

1.  **Dumbbell/Barbell Bench Press:** 4 sets x 8-12 reps
2.  **Incline Dumbbell Press:** 3 sets x 10-12 reps
3.  **Cable Flys / Pec Deck:** 3 sets x 15 reps
4.  **Tricep Pushdowns (Rope):** 4 sets x 12-15 reps
5.  **Overhead Tricep Extension:** 3 sets x 12 reps
6.  **Abs:** Hanging Leg Raises (3 x 12), Plank (3 x 45-60 sec)

### Tuesday: Back, Biceps, Cardio

1.  **Lat Pulldowns (Wide Grip):** 4 sets x 10-12 reps
2.  **Seated Cable Rows:** 3 sets x 10-12 reps
3.  **Face Pulls:** 3 sets x 15 reps (Good for posture)
4.  **Barbell/Dumbbell Curls:** 4 sets x 10-12 reps
5.  **Hammer Curls:** 3 sets x 12 reps
6.  **Cardio:** 20 mins Incline Walk (Treadmill: Max incline, Speed 3.5-4.5)

### Wednesday: Legs, Shoulders

1.  **Squats (Goblet or Barbell):** 4 sets x 8-10 reps
2.  **Leg Press:** 3 sets x 12 reps
3.  **Romanian Deadlifts (Hamstrings):** 3 sets x 10-12 reps
4.  **Overhead Press (Seated or Standing):** 4 sets x 8-12 reps
5.  **Lateral Raises:** 4 sets x 15 reps (Burner)
6.  **Calf Raises:** 4 sets x 15-20 reps

### Thursday: Active Recovery & Abs

1.  **Cardio:** 45 mins steady state (Jogging, Elliptical, or Fast Walk).
2.  **Abs Circuit (3 rounds):**
    - Crunches x 20
    - Russian Twists x 20
    - Mountain Climbers x 30 sec
    - Plank x 1 min

### Friday: Full Body Intensity

1.  **Deadlifts:** 3 sets x 6-8 reps
2.  **Dumbbell Shoulder Press:** 3 sets x 10 reps
3.  **Lunges:** 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
4.  **Pull-ups (or Assisted):** 3 sets x Max reps
5.  **Dips:** 3 sets x Max reps

### Saturday: Cardio Blitz

- **HIIT:** 20 mins (30 sec sprint / 30 sec rest) OR 1 hour active sports/hiking.
