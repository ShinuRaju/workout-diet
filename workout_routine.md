# Workout Routine (6:00 AM Start)

**Focus:** Fat Loss + Muscle Retention. Keep rest periods short (45-60 sec) to keep heart rate up.

- **Wed:** Legs + Shoulders

- **Fri:** Cult.fit Lower Body Strength (100 pushups at home before class)

## Guidelines

- **Warm-up:** 5 mins brisk walk/light jog + Dynamic stretching.
- **Warm-up Sets:** For compound lifts (Bench, Squat, Deadlift, OHP), do 2-3 ramp-up sets (e.g., empty bar x 10, 50% x 8, 75% x 5) before working sets.
- **Pre-Workout (Mon/Tue/Wed):** 500 skips (~5 mins) as dynamic warm-up before gym.
- **Pre-Workout (Thu/Fri â€” Cult.fit days):** 100 pushups at home before class. No skipping.
- **Sunday:** Complete rest. No training.
- **Cardio:** Do 15-20 mins _after_ weights, not before (save energy for lifting).
- **Progressive Overload:** When you hit the top of your rep range on all sets, increase weight by 2.5-5 lbs next session. Track every session.
- **Mind-Muscle Connection:** Focus on controlled eccentrics (2-3 sec lowering phase), especially on isolation moves.

## Detailed Exercises

### Monday: Chest, Triceps, Abs

1.  **Dumbbell/Barbell Bench Press:** 4 sets x 8-12 reps
2.  **Incline Dumbbell Press:** 3 sets x 10-12 reps
3.  **Cable Flys / Pec Deck:** 3 sets x 15 reps
4.  **Tricep Pushdowns (Rope):** 4 sets x 12-15 reps
5.  **Overhead Tricep Extension:** 3 sets x 12 reps
6.  **Abs:** Hanging Leg Raises (3 x 12), Plank (3 x 45-60 sec)

### Tuesday: Back, Biceps, Cardio

1.  **Lat Pulldowns (Wide Grip):** 4 sets x 10-12 reps
2.  **Seated Cable Rows:** 3 sets x 10-12 reps
3.  **Face Pulls:** 3 sets x 15 reps (Good for posture)
4.  **Barbell/Dumbbell Curls:** 4 sets x 10-12 reps
5.  **Hammer Curls:** 3 sets x 12 reps
6.  **Cardio:** 20 mins Incline Walk (Treadmill: Max incline, Speed 3.5-4.5)

### Wednesday: Legs, Shoulders

1.  **Squats (Goblet or Barbell):** 4 sets x 8-10 reps
2.  **Leg Press:** 3 sets x 12 reps
3.  **Romanian Deadlifts (Hamstrings):** 3 sets x 10-12 reps
4.  **Overhead Press (Seated or Standing):** 4 sets x 8-12 reps
5.  **Lateral Raises:** 4 sets x 15 reps (Burner)
6.  **Reverse Flys / Rear Delt Machine:** 3 sets x 15 reps (Shoulder balance)
7.  **Leg Curls:** 3 sets x 12-15 reps (Hamstring isolation)
8.  **Calf Raises:** 4 sets x 15-20 reps

### Thursday: Cult.fit Upper Body Strength

**At home (before class):**

- 100 Pushups (break into 5 x 20 or 10 x 10)

**At Cult.fit:**

- Follow the coached Upper Body Strength class
- Focus on form cues from the trainer

### Friday: Cult.fit Lower Body Strength

**At home (before class):**

- 100 Pushups (break into 5 x 20 or 10 x 10)

**At Cult.fit:**

- Follow the coached Lower Body Strength class
- Focus on depth, form, and mind-muscle connection

### Saturday: Endurance + Bodyweight

1.  **Skipping:** 2000 reps (break into 4 x 500 with 60 sec rest)
2.  **Incline Treadmill Walk:** 30 mins at 5 km/h, max incline
3.  **Pushups:** 50 reps (5 x 10)
4.  **Dips:** 50 reps (5 x 10)
5.  **Pull-ups:** 25 reps (5 x 5)
6.  **Chin-ups:** 25 reps (5 x 5)

## Recovery & Lifestyle (Non-Negotiables)

- **Sleep:** 7-9 hours. This is when muscle repairs. No compromise.
- **Hydration:** Minimum 3-4 liters/day. Add electrolytes on training days.
- **Stretching/Mobility:** 10 mins post-workout static stretching. Foam roll tight areas.
- **Deload Week:** Every 4-6 weeks, reduce weights by 40-50% for one week. Prevents burnout & injury.
